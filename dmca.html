Copyright Protection

PNGfam respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects users to do the same. We comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) and will promptly remove content that violates copyright laws.

Reporting Copyright Infringement

If you are a copyright owner or authorized representative and believe that any content on PNGfam infringes your copyright, you may submit a DMCA notice requesting removal of the material.

Required Information for DMCA Notice

  • Your full name and contact information
  • Proof of ownership of the copyrighted work
  • The exact URL of the infringing content
  • A statement confirming good faith belief of infringement
  • A statement that the information provided is accurate

Content Removal Process

Upon receiving a valid DMCA complaint, we will review the request and remove or disable access to the infringing material where appropriate.

Repeat Infringers

Users who repeatedly upload copyrighted content without permission may have their access restricted or permanently blocked.
